About Trashy

Trashy was made to make locating trash cans easier. It uses geolocation to find your position and displays the nearest 20 trash cans to you. Users who sign in via Google can also submit trash cans. These trash cans can be seen by anyone, but users who submit them can see which ones are theirs by the green markers.
